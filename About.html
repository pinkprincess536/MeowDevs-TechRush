About Us

We exist to make giving more meaningful. By connecting generous hearts with genuine needs, we strive to ensure that every act of kindness creates real, lasting change. Our vision is a world where no help goes unheard, and no need goes unmet.

Our Mission

At JOY, we believe that every act of kindness has the power to transform lives. Our mission is to create a seamless bridge between generous donors and those in need, making the process of giving as simple and impactful as possible.
